Somali Mental Health: One Story of Hope



Analysis: The video above addresses the issue of containing/imprisoning “abnormal” or “mentally ill” people. It is an issue that exists across many societies and can be seen in many forms (e.g. psychiatric wards). The video touches on containment via chains, but we can still generalize the issue as containment issue instead of focusing too much on the chains.
        Although a case can be made for containment preventing self-harm and harm to other members of society, the principle power-dynamic still remains and can be critiqued. That is to say, normal people decide what “normal” is and use it as a parameter. Those outside of that parameter are deemed “abnormal” and are thus contained. Thus, there is an asymmetrical power dynamic between the normal and abnormal and it is that very power dynamic that I will try to critique in my comic.
