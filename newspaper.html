Mental Health: Last Week Tonight with John Oliver (HBO)



Analysis: John Oliver in this video address how politicians avoid having meaningful and constructive discussions on gun control and instead frame the issue of gun control as a mental health issue. So, a mass shooting is characterized as mental health issue; the logic here is, the perpetrator of the mass shooting is mentally ill, gun control has nothing to do with this. In the same breath, mental health policy is not actually dealt with. That is, there is no change on the policy, funding, framing. Using mental health as a scapegoat (for political reasons) actually makes dealing with mental health at a macro and at a micro level more difficult, as the narrative of mental health is further reified as violent. It is this politically motivated reality that I try to critique by revealing a future (via newspaper) in which, although the number of mental health activists has significantly increased, the construct of people with mental health issues as violent remains intact in mainstream media.
